support you need, every step of the way. From your first inquiry to final delivery, we’re here
to make your experience seamless and stress-free.

Step 1: Inquiry
• How It Works:
o Visit our website and explore our services.
o Fill out the contact form or reach out via email/phone to share your
requirements.
• What Happens Next:
o Our team will respond within 24 hours to discuss your needs and
recommend the best package for you.
Step 2: Consultation
• How It Works:
o Book a free consultation through our integrated booking system.
o Choose a date and time that works for you.
• What Happens Next:
o During the consultation, we’ll understand your career goals, review your
current CV (if applicable), and outline how we can help.
Step 3: Package Selection
• How It Works:
o Based on your consultation, select the package that best suits your needs
(Gold, Platinum, or Diamond).
o Confirm your choice and proceed to payment.
• What Happens Next:
o You’ll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your selected
package.
Step 4: Collaboration & Delivery
• How It Works:
o Share your career history, achievements, and target roles with our team.
o Our experts will craft your CV, cover letter, and/or LinkedIn profile (depending
on your package).
• What Happens Next:
o You’ll receive the first draft within 4 working days.
o Provide feedback, and we’ll make unlimited revisions for 6 months.
Step 5: Success
• How It Works:
o Use your new CV, cover letter, and LinkedIn profile to apply for jobs with
confidence.
• What Happens Next:

About Us
How We Started
Pioneering HR Transformation Since 2021
In 2021, Precious Lunga founded Orancy Planet HR Consultancy with a bold vision: to
revolutionize HR functions and drive transformative change. With nearly two decades of
experience in senior Human Resources roles, Precious identified a critical need for a
consultancy that could seamlessly bridge human capital and technological advancement.
The name ‘Orancy Planet’ reflects our mission to create a supportive, empowering
environment for every individual and organization we serve.
Our Approach
At Orancy Planet, we integrate cutting-edge HR strategies with transformative change
management practices. Our tailored solutions are designed to:
• Optimize human capital by unlocking the full potential of your workforce.
• Drive organizational change through innovative, data-driven methodologies.
Today, our team of HR experts partners with businesses across the UK, leveraging their
deep expertise to foster growth, streamline processes, and build empowered, high-
performing teams.
Our Commitment to the Future
As Orancy Planet continues to grow, we remain dedicated to pioneering new HR
methodologies and technologies. By staying ahead of industry trends and embracing
innovation, we empower our clients to navigate complex business landscapes with
confidence.
