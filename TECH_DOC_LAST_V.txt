About Us
How We Started
Pioneering HR Transformation Since 2021
Founded in 2021 by Precious Lunga (fondly known as Lady Presh), Orancy Planet is a
global consultancy and innovation hub specializing in HR transformation, IT solutions, and
sustainable business growth. With a deep passion for people, cultures, and evolving
technology, Lady Presh has built Orancy Planet to connect with individuals and
organizations from all walks of life, creating meaningful impacts in the world of HR and
beyond.
A natural leader and advocate for equality, Lady Presh’s personal experiences have shaped
her unwavering commitment to fairness, compassion, and humanity. Holding dual degrees
in Business and Management from Oxford Brookes University and being a proud member of
the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD), she brings over a decade of
expertise in business development, change management, and global project execution
across industries such as insurance, financial services, automotive, and humanitarian
initiatives.
At Orancy Planet, we integrate cutting-edge HR strategies with transformative change
management practices to optimize human capital and drive organizational success. Our
tailored solutions empower businesses across the UK and globally, helping them build
high-performing teams, streamline processes, and foster cultures of growth and inclusion.
Guided by our core values—Innovation, Client-Centricity, Excellence, and
Compassionate Leadership—we strive to be the trusted partner of choice for businesses
and individuals. Our mission is to deliver innovative, high-tech solutions that enhance
operational efficiency, drive competitiveness, and support sustainable growth, ensuring no
one is left behind in the digital age.
Lady Presh’s vision extends to fostering high-tech innovation in emerging economies,
particularly in Africa, bridging the gap between global advancements and local needs.
Through strategic partnerships and a commitment to diversity, Orancy Planet is shaping an
inclusive, prosperous, and sustainable future for businesses and communities worldwide.
Together, we help our clients navigate complex business landscapes while fostering an
empowered, inclusive environment. Let us help you say with confidence, "The HR and Tech
aspects of my business? We’ve got it seamlessly covered!"
