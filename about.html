How We Started

Pioneering HR Transformation Since 2021

In 2021, Precious Lunga founded Orancy Planet HR Consultancy with a bold vision: to revolutionize HR functions and drive transformative change. With nearly two decades of experience in senior Human Resources roles, Precious identified a critical need for a consultancy that could seamlessly bridge human capital and technological advancement.

The name 'Orancy Planet' reflects our mission to create a supportive, empowering environment for every individual and organization we serve.

Our Approach

At Orancy Planet, we integrate cutting-edge HR strategies with transformative change management practices. Our tailored solutions are designed to:

  • Optimize human capital by unlocking the full potential of your workforce.
  • Drive organizational change through innovative, data-driven methodologies.

Today, our team of HR experts partners with businesses across the UK, leveraging their deep expertise to foster growth, streamline processes, and build empowered, high-performing teams.

Our Commitment to the Future

As Orancy Planet continues to grow, we remain dedicated to pioneering new HR methodologies and technologies. By staying ahead of industry trends and embracing innovation, we empower our clients to navigate complex business landscapes with confidence.

Our Mission

Empowering Businesses, Transforming Futures

We aim to empower businesses worldwide by delivering innovative, high-tech solutions that enhance operational efficiency, drive competitiveness, and support sustainable growth. We make technology accessible to all, ensuring no one is left behind as the world embraces the digital age.
