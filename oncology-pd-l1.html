Sof PD-L1: AI-Powered PD-L1
Quantification for Immunotherapy.

Accurate, efficient, and objective PD-L1 scoring for immuno-oncology. Powered by AI, trusted by pathologists.

Close to the Ground Truth

Trained with 1M+ cell annotations by 150+ pathologists worldwide, Sof PD-L1 delivers analysis results that are close to the ground truth.

Reduced Discrepancy

Objective results reduce interpretation discrepancy between pathologists. Consensus rate increased from 81.4% to 90.2% with AI assistance. (Choi et al., Eur J Cancer 2022)

Patient Enrichment

With 96% accuracy, Sof PD-L1 finds 50% more patients eligible for immunotherapy compared to human-classified PD-L1(-) groups. AI accurately predicted and rescued ICI responders with TPS < 1% by pathologists.
