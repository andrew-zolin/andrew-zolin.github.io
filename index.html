About Orancy Planet

Empowering businesses with innovative HR solutions and technology integration

Transforming HR for the Digital Age

In 2021, Precious Lunga founded Orancy Planet HR Consultancy with a bold vision: to revolutionize HR functions and drive transformative change. With nearly two decades of experience in senior Human Resources roles, Precious identified a critical need for a consultancy that could seamlessly bridge human capital and technological advancement. The name ‘Orancy Planet’ reflects our mission to create a supportive, empowering environment for every individual and organization we serve.
