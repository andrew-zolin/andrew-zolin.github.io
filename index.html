About Orancy Planet

Pioneering HR Transformation Since 2021

Founded by Lady Presh

Founded in 2021 by Precious Lunga (fondly known as Lady Presh), Orancy Planet is a global consultancy and innovation hub specializing in HR transformation, IT solutions, and sustainable business growth. With a deep passion for people, cultures, and evolving technology, Lady Presh has built Orancy Planet to connect with individuals and organizations from all walks of life, creating meaningful impacts in the world of HR and beyond.

Our Standards

We operate with the highest standards of professionalism and ethics

Integrity

We are committed to conducting our business with the highest levels of Integrity. Transparency, honesty, and ethical decision-making guide every aspect of our operations, ensuring that we build trust with our clients, partners, and the communities we serve. In our humanitarian collaborations, we adhere to strict ethical standards, ensuring that the technology we develop and implement is used to protect the dignity and rights of vulnerable populations.

Diversity

Diversity is fundamental to our approach to business and innovation. We embrace diverse perspectives, cultures, and ideas, recognising that inclusivity is key to driving creativity and developing solutions that cater to a global audience. Our commitment to diversity extends to our humanitarian projects, where we work with a wide range of stakeholders—local communities, governments, and international organisations—to ensure that our solutions reflect the needs and values of diverse populations.
