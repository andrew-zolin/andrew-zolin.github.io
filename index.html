Have Any Questions?

Find answers to frequently asked questions about SOF AI technology

How does SOF AI improve radiologist workflow?

SOF AI assists radiologists by automatically detecting abnormalities in medical images, prioritizing cases based on urgency, and providing detailed analysis that helps reduce reading time while improving accuracy. This allows radiologists to focus on the most critical cases and make faster, more informed decisions.

What types of medical images can SOF AI analyze?

SOF AI is designed to analyze chest X-rays (CXR) and mammography (MMG) images, detecting a wide range of conditions including nodules, consolidation, pneumothorax, and more with high accuracy rates of 97-99%. Our technology is continuously expanding to support additional imaging modalities.

How accurate is SOF AI in detecting abnormalities?

SOF AI achieves 97-99% accuracy in detecting abnormalities on chest X-rays and mammograms. Our algorithms have been trained on diverse datasets and validated through rigorous clinical testing to ensure reliable performance across different patient populations and imaging equipment.

How does SOF AI integrate with existing hospital systems?

SOF AI seamlessly integrates with existing PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication Systems) and other hospital information systems through standard DICOM and HL7 protocols. Our solution can be deployed on-premises or via secure cloud infrastructure, depending on your facility's requirements.
