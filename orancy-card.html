Orancy Card

The Orancy Card is a premium loyalty program designed to reward our most dedicated clients. Inspired by the concept of exclusivity and value, the Orancy Card offers a range of benefits to clients who demonstrate long-term commitment, consistency, and contribution to our growth.

Features of the Orancy Card

1. Eligibility

  • Available to loyal clients who have been with Orancy Planet for a minimum period (e.g., 1 year).
  • Clients on retainer agreements who maintain consistent payments.
  • Clients who refer new business and contribute to Orancy Planet's growth.

2. Tiers

  • Silver Tier: For clients with 1+ year of loyalty or consistent retainer payments.
  • Gold Tier: For clients with 2+ years of loyalty or significant retainer contributions.
  • Platinum Tier: For top-tier clients with 3+ years of loyalty, high retainer payments, or substantial referrals.

Benefits of the Orancy Card

For All Cardholders:

1. Exclusive Discounts

  • 10-20% discounts on all Orancy Planet services (e.g., HR consulting, training programs, technology solutions).
  • Discounts increase with higher tiers (e.g., Silver: 10%, Gold: 15%, Platinum: 20%).

2. Priority Support

  • Dedicated account manager for personalized service.
  • 24/7 priority access to customer support and advisory services.

3. Free Consultations

  • Quarterly free consultations with HR and business development experts.
  • Access to exclusive webinars and workshops.

4. Event Invitations

  • Complimentary tickets to Orancy Planet's exclusive events, such as the HR Innovation Summit and Tech Transformation Conference.
  • VIP access to networking events with industry leaders.

5. Customized Solutions

  • Tailored HR and technology solutions designed specifically for cardholders' business needs.

For Gold and Platinum Cardholders:

6. Extended Revisions

  • Unlimited revisions on HR documents, CVs, and cover letters for 12 months.

7. Complimentary Services

  • Free CV optimization or LinkedIn profile review annually.
  • One free coaching session (personal or executive) per year.

8. Early Access to New Products

  • Be the first to access and test new Orancy Planet services and technology solutions.

For Platinum Cardholders Only:

9. Elite Networking Opportunities

  • Invitations to private roundtable discussions with Lady Presh and other industry leaders.
  • Opportunities to collaborate on exclusive projects with Orancy Planet.

10. Annual Business Review

  • A comprehensive business health check and strategy session with Orancy Planet's senior consultants.

11. Charity Donations in Your Name

  • Orancy Planet will make a donation to a charity of your choice annually, reflecting your commitment to social impact.

How to Earn the Orancy Card

  • Loyalty: Maintain a long-term relationship with Orancy Planet.
  • Retainer Consistency: Consistently pay for retainer services without delays.
  • Referrals: Bring new clients to Orancy Planet and contribute to our growth.
