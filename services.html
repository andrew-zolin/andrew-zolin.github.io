Customer Journey & Buying Process

Your Path to Success Starts Here

At Orancy Planet, we've designed a simple and transparent process to ensure you get the support you need, every step of the way. From your first inquiry to final delivery, we're here to make your experience seamless and stress-free.

Step-by-Step Guide from Inquiry to Purchase

Customer Journey on Buying from a Website

1

Website Entry

Action: Customer lands on the website via search engine, referral, or direct visit.

Key Design Elements:

  • Landing Page: Clear navigation, search bar, and product categories should be visible.
  • Personalization: Use personalized suggestions based on customer behavior or previous visits.
  • Responsiveness: Ensure that the website is mobile-friendly.
2

Product Discovery

Action: Customer searches or browses the product catalog.

Key Design Elements:

  • Product Filters: Provide filters like price, category, popularity, etc.
  • Search Optimization: The search function should provide relevant results with suggestions.
  • Product Detail Page: Include high-quality images, detailed descriptions, pricing, and reviews.
3

Product Selection

Action: Customer clicks on a product, reviews the details, and adds it to the cart.

Key Design Elements:

  • Add to Cart/Buy Now: Prominent buttons that lead to the next steps.
  • Stock Availability: Real-time updates on stock levels.
  • Cross-selling: Suggest related or complementary products.
4

Shopping Cart Review

Action: Customer reviews the items in their shopping cart.

Key Design Elements:

  • Cart Summary: Display products, quantity, pricing, and any applicable discounts.
  • Shipping Cost Estimate: Display shipping cost based on the customer's location.
  • Coupon Code Entry: Allow users to apply discount codes if applicable.
5

Checkout Process

Action: Customer proceeds to checkout.

Key Design Elements:

  • Guest Checkout: Allow customers to check out without needing to create an account.
  • Account Login Option: Option for registered users to log in and access saved preferences.
  • Shipping Information: Collect shipping details in a clear, simple form.
  • Billing Information: Secure collection of payment details (credit card, PayPal, etc.).
  • Shipping Method: Allow users to select from multiple shipping options with delivery times and costs.
  • Order Summary: Final order details before confirming the purchase.
6

Order Confirmation

Action: Customer submits the order and receives confirmation.

Key Design Elements:

  • Order Confirmation Page: Display order number, details, and estimated delivery time.
  • Email Confirmation: Automatically send an order confirmation email with all relevant details.
  • Customer Account (Optional): If they created an account, allow them to track the order via the website.

Order Process Design (Back-End)

1

Order Submission

Action: Once the customer submits the order, the system captures the details.

Key Design Elements:

  • Order Number Generation: Each order gets a unique order ID for tracking.
  • Integration with Payment Gateway: Verify and process payment through a secure gateway.
  • Inventory Check: Confirm stock availability before processing the order.
4

Delivery and Post-Purchase

Action: The customer receives the product.

Key Design Elements:

  • Delivery Updates: Send periodic updates on delivery status.
  • Order Tracking: Customers should be able to track their package from their account.
  • Post-Purchase Support: Provide easy access to customer service for inquiries or returns.
5

Feedback and Returns

Action: After delivery, collect feedback and manage returns if necessary.

Key Design Elements:

  • Feedback Request: Send an automated email or prompt to leave a review or feedback.
  • Return Process: Simplify returns by providing return labels and step-by-step instructions for returning the product.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Provide options for refunds, exchanges, or credits.

Process Map for Customer Purchase and Order Process

Swimlane 1: Customer Actions

  • 1 Website Entry
    • The customer accesses the website (mobile or desktop).
  • 2 Product Discovery
    • Customer searches for products or browses through categories.
    • Filters or search options narrow down preferences.
  • 3 Product Selection
    • The customer selects the product, views details, and adds it to the shopping cart.
  • 4 Shopping Cart Review
    • Reviews the cart, checks items, pricing, and applies any discount codes.
  • 5 Checkout Process
    • Enters shipping information, selects payment method, and confirms the order.
  • 6 Order Confirmation
    • Receives confirmation of the order on-screen and via email.

Swimlane 2: System/Backend Actions

  • 1 Order Submission
    • The system generates a unique order ID and confirms stock availability.
    • Payment gateway processes payment.
  • 2 Order Processing
    • Inventory levels are updated, and warehouse/fulfillment team receives the order.
  • 3 Order Fulfillment
    • The warehouse team prepares and ships the product.
    • A tracking number is generated, and the customer is notified of shipment.
  • 4 Delivery
    • The shipping carrier delivers the package to the customer.
    • Tracking is available to the customer throughout the process.
  • 5 Post-Purchase Support
    • Customer receives an option for feedback.
    • System handles returns and refunds, if applicable.
